# Questionnaires

## Why we're asking

These three short questionnaires are the research instrument for a paper on how exposure to local LLMs shifts AI literacy, agency, and competency among design researchers. Responses across the three stages are analyzed using **UMAP** (McInnes et al. 2020) to visualize movement across the dataset and **Top2Vec** (Angelov 2020) to surface the latent semantic structure of the free-text reflections. Results are intended for the DRS digital library.

The questionnaire is structured around three dimensions:

- **Agency** — who is in control of your tools, your data, your outputs
- **Materiality** — how you weigh the ethical and environmental costs of AI use
- **Competency** — what you can actually do with AI in your research practice

Each dimension is probed with a paired structured (Likert-style) and unstructured (free-text) item.

## The three stages

| Stage | When | Length | What it covers |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Pre-workshop** | Before the session | ~5 min | Baseline: where you are now |
| **Post-workshop** | Immediately after the session | ~5 min | Shift: what changed in 90 minutes |
| **1-week follow-up** | 7 days later, by email | ~10 min | Stickiness: what made it back into your practice |

## Transparency

The full set of 18 items is mirrored in [`questions.md`](questions.md) in this folder, so you can read everything in plain text before responding. We kept this mirror separate from the forms so the instrument survives if the Google Forms are ever taken down, and so the analysis can be reproduced.

## Consent and opt-out

- Participation is **voluntary**. You can attend the workshop without completing any questionnaire.
- You can skip any individual question.
- Responses can be anonymized at your request — contact Ben.
- If you want your responses excluded from the paper after the fact, email Ben within four weeks of the workshop.

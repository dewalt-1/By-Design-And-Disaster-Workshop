# Questionnaire — full items

All 18 items across three stages, three dimensions, two types. Mirrored from the Google Forms for transparency and archival.

## Pre-Workshop

| Dimension | Type | Content |
|---|---|---|
| Agency | Unstructured | Describe a recent instance where you used AI for research. Who was truly in control of the output: you or the corporate interface? |
| Agency | Structured | I feel I have full sovereignty over the tools and data I use in my research process. |
| Materiality | Unstructured | How do you currently weigh the environmental and ethical costs of AI against the convenience of using cloud services? |
| Materiality | Structured | I actively prioritize the ecological footprint of my digital tools when choosing which ones to use. |
| Competency | Unstructured | Describe your ability to translate messy, material design experiments into structured academic text using digital assistants. |
| Competency | Structured | I am confident in my ability to use AI as a craft tool to clarify my own tacit knowledge. |

## Post-Workshop

| Dimension | Type | Content |
|---|---|---|
| Agency | Unstructured | Now that the model is running on your physical hardware, how has your sense of authorship over your research data shifted? |
| Agency | Structured | I feel significantly more in control of my research now that I am not using third-party servers. |
| Materiality | Unstructured | Reflecting on today's session, how does the ability to run models locally change your perception of AI's environmental impact? |
| Materiality | Structured | I believe that local AI is a more ethical choice for my specific research practice than cloud AI. |
| Competency | Unstructured | How has today's hands-on setup improved your ability to handle sensitive field notes or proprietary design information? |
| Competency | Structured | I am now confident in setting up local models, system prompts, and folder structures on my own. |

## 1-Week Follow-up

| Dimension | Type | Content |
|---|---|---|
| Agency | Unstructured | Reflect on your research tasks this week. In what specific moments did you choose local AI over cloud AI to maintain your agency? |
| Agency | Structured | In my daily work, I consistently choose tools that allow me to maintain full sovereignty over my data. |
| Materiality | Unstructured | Have you changed any of your research habits this week to align more closely with your ethical or environmental values? |
| Materiality | Structured | The ecological and ethical cost of a tool is now a primary factor in my research workflow. |
| Competency | Unstructured | Describe how you used local AI this week as a "craft tool" to refine your writing practice rather than just an automated solution. |
| Competency | Structured | I have successfully integrated local LLMs into my iterative writing and research translation process. |

## Notes

- Structured items use a Likert scale (e.g., Strongly disagree → Strongly agree, 5 points).
- Unstructured items are free-text and feed the Top2Vec analysis.
- Items are paired by dimension across stages so movement can be tracked at the individual level.
